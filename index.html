Butterfly Steps to Calm Down

  • Pause & Land Gently
    → Like a butterfly landing softly on a flower.
  • Breathe Like a Flutter
    → Slow breaths in… slow breaths out… 🌸 Feel your wings calm.
  • Pick a Quiet Activity
    → Color, hum, squeeze, or close your eyes.
  • Let Your Feelings Settle
    → Like pollen floating down — gently and safely.
  • Fly Back Calmly
    → Ready to join again, light and peaceful.

Zones

Blue Zone

Sad • Hurt • Sick • Tired
What can I do?
REST
Take a break • Ask for help • Talk to someone
🦋

Green Zone

Happy • Focused
Calm • Excited
What can I do?
GO
Finish my work • Help others • Share ideas
🦋

Orange Zone

Surprised • Confused
Worried • Silly
What can I do?
SLOW DOWN
Take deep breaths • Talk to someone • Count to ten
🦋

Red Zone

Angry • Raging
Terrified • Annoyed
What can I do?
STOP
Take a time out • Run a lap • Squeeze a stress ball • Drink water
